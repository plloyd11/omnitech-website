Turnkey Solutions Provider

100% In-House Resources

Engineering

Our Team of Automation Engineers provide in-house machine design services and technical support.

Fabrication

A full machine shop with CNC machining, turning, grinding and welding compliments an Assembly Department where technicians assemble and debug equipment.

Installation

Automation Technicians assist with the installation, set-up, and start-up of every piece of equipment the company builds.

Budgetary Estimate

Omnitech provides a quick and free budgetary estimate of the cost and lead time for a project so a customer can analyze a project’s Return on Investment (ROI) before requesting a formal proposal from Omnitech or submitting a Capital Appropriation Request (CAR) to their company’s management.

Concept Development

Omnitech’s automation concepts are developed by a team that draws on over 100 years of automation experience. The goal is to identify the most robust, cost-effective solution, while identifying areas of risk.

Proposal Generation

Our proposals are detailed and highly-technical:

  • 2D CAD Concept Drawing
  • Functional Requirements & Design Specifications
  • Pricing, lead time, and payment terms
  • Gantt Chart with project milestones

Mechanical Design

Omnitech’s Mechanical Design Team utilizes leading-edge, industry-standard 3D modeling software to design every part and assembly for a system.

Omnitech's Team draws on its vast automation experience to develop solutions to even the most difficult automation challenges.

Controls - Electrical

The entire controls design, programming and electrical wiring process is managed by a team of in-house resources.

Fabrication – Assembly

In-house machinists, toolmakers, and assembly technicians provide fabrication services for in-house automation projects and customers in need of individual machined parts, fixtures and assemblies.

  • 3D CAM Machining
  • Turning
  • Welding
  • Grinding
  • Assembly

Project Management

As engineers, we’re fanatical about process and documentation. We use an ERP system and a sophisticated project management process, to manage every step of developing new equipment. And, we update our customers on the status of their project every two weeks with Project Status Reports and Gantt Charts.

Equipment Installation – Training

An in-house Automation Services Team coordinates the installation of every machine Omnitech develops. We also provide training for Operators and Maintenance Employees.

Service

We service the equipment we sell. Whether it’s new or 25-years-old, our Automation Services Team can provide repair, machine upgrade, and Preventative Maintenance services. We’re your partner for the lifetime you own an Omnitech machine!
