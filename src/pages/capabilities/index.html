Controls Systems

Whether your application requires developing a new controls system complete with a Programmable Logic Controller (PLC), Human Machine Interface (HMI) and electrical enclosure, we have the experience and resources. We can also repair, reprogram and rewire older legacy systems. Need data exporting or remote access capability? No problem!

Work Cells - Small Tools

Need to increase production, improve product quality, or have an easy way to test your product? Omnitech can design and build anything from a simple tool to fixture your part, a test nest for part inspection, or a semi-automated work cell. With in-house design, fabrication, and controls, no project is too simple or complex.

Robotic Systems

We’ve been integrating Cartesian, Scara and Articulating robots for over 25 years.

Vision Systems

Most automated system Omnitech develops include vision technology. Whether it’s a simple sensor, a cost-effective b/w camera, or a full-color HD multi-camera system, we can design, program and install it.
